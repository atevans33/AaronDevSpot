Our Story

Alice and Ruben Guijarro

Alice and Ruben's love story began unexpectedly at a quaint café nestled in the heart of the Culver City. Alice, an aspiring artist, was sketching the bustling streets outside when Ruben, a charming writer, stumbled upon her work. Intrigued by her talent, he struck up a conversation, and their connection was instant. Over cups of steaming coffee, they shared stories, dreams, and laughter, discovering a deep bond that would shape their future.

Their relationship blossomed with each passing day, marked by countless adventures, and shared passions. From hiking through southern California’s highest mountains to stargazing on secluded beaches, Alice and Ruben found joy in exploring the world together. Through ups and downs, they remained steadfast companions, their love growing stronger with each passing moment. As they navigated life's challenges hand in hand, they knew they were meant to spend eternity together.

With hearts full of love and excitement, Alice and Ruben now embark on the journey towards their wedding day. Amidst the whirlwind of planning, they moment is cherish as they savor the anticipation of exchanging vows and beginning their next chapter as husband and wife. We hope you join us amidst family and friends on this upcoming day of a lifetime, as we promise to love and support each other for all eternity.
