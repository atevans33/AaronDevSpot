  • I spoke of

    Beautiful Sunsets

    into the wind...

    I saw a sunset yesterday,
    but I thought it could be more lovely.
    I went ahead and sent a letter up to God titled,
    “A few ways to make your sky more beautiful.”

    It was a photo of you.

    I just wanted to let you know.
    In case you find angels falling for you.
    I’m sure they’ll want to see someone so beautiful in person.

  • I spoke of

    A Thousand Words

    into the wind...

    I don’t believe it possible to paint a picture that can hold your beauty.

    But if a picture is worth a thousand words,
    Then how about a thousand poems?
    A thousand parables?
    A thousand proclamations?

    All pertaining to the impossible task
    of capturing God’s most beautiful creation

  • I spoke of

    Lighthouse Love

    into the wind...

    No matter how far or wide you go, I promise to wait you.
    Like a meadow of flowers, I pray the sun holds me company as I stand here for you.
    No moonlit sonnets for today, I’m tired of seeing the sun set when I’m with you.

    I pray it holds it’s shine forever.
    I pray it never ceases.
    A candle wick love that lasts an eternity.

    I promise to hold the sun on my shoulders so you can always find your way back to me.
    A lighthouse love

    That promises:
    No matter how dark the dusk
    No matter how deep the sea
    No matter how difficult it can be to see a way out of everything

    You’ll always find me
    And we’ll always find our way back to each other
    And we’ll always have
    One another

  • I spoke of

    Summer Days

    into the wind...

    I wrote a list of everything lovely about you

    Before tearing it into a thousand pieces and tossing it into the wind.

    So every time you feel a cool breeze that makes the warmest day lovely.

    Know, I taught the wind what it felt like to spend time with you.

  • I spoke of

    I spoke Into the Wind...

    If you are going about your day
    And you feel a warmth in the wind
    Or a beautiful description about your surroundings
    Or God’s presence over your shoulder

    It’s probably my fault.
    What I’m trying to say is

    If you feel love in the air
    It’s because I stood atop the highest point I could find
    And spoke about you into the wind

    I spoke of Sunsets and Parables,
    Lighthouses and cool summer days too

    In hopes the breeze would find my words lovely enough to take with it
    In hopes the birds would be my first listeners
    In hopes they would travel the world
    In hopes they would find the source of their inspiration
    In hopes they would find you
