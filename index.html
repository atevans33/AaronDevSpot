Digital Accessibility

Click the highlighted buttons below to see visual examples of the remediation work that I offer.

With up to a quarter of US adults having some sort of disability, it's important to ensure your web documents are accessible. I have remediated thousands of documents to meet Section 508 standards. Whether it’s PDFs, Word Documents, PowerPoints, or videos, your content will be accessible and usable.

I offer synchronous and asynchronous training for content remediation. Trainings come with reference documents and/ or webpages for later use by trained individuals.

What is Section 508?

In 1998, Congress amended the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 to require federal agencies to make their electronic and information technology (EIT) accessible to people with disabilities. The law applies to all federal agencies when they develop, procure, maintain, or use electronic and information technology.
