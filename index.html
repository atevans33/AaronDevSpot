Digital Accessibility

Click the highlighted buttons to see visual examples of the Digital Accessibility and the remediation work that I offer.

With up to a quarter of US adults having some sort of disability, it's important to ensure your web documents are accessible. I have remediated thousands of documents to meet Section 508 standards. Many of these have been in the realm of education and real estate, ensuring a company’s digital presence is protected and accessible. Whether it’s PDFs, Word Documents, PowerPoints, or videos, your content will be accessible and usable.

I offer synchronous and asynchronous training for document remediation . Trainings come with reference documents and/ or webpages for later use by trained individuals.

What is Section 508?

In 1998, Congress amended the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 to require federal agencies to make their electronic and information technology (EIT) accessible to people with disabilities. The law applies to all federal agencies when they develop, procure, maintain, or use electronic and information technology.
